Is the printing really inkless?
Yes. PocketPrint uses thermal printing technology — heat activates the special paper to create the image. No cartridges, no refills, no mess. Ever.
Will it work with my phone?
If your phone runs iOS 14+ or Android 9+, you're set. The free PocketPrint app handles everything via Bluetooth 5.0 — no Wi-Fi or accounts required.
How long does the paper last?
Our premium thermal rolls last 5+ years when stored in normal conditions, and 10+ years for our archival paper add-on. Keep them out of direct sunlight for best results.
Where do you ship?
Worldwide. Shipping is free on every order. Most countries see delivery within 3–5 business days — every package is tracked and insured.
What's the print resolution?
203 DPI — sharp enough for photos, dense enough for text, fast enough that you'll forget you're waiting.
Can I print in color?
Standard rolls print in monochrome (which honestly looks gorgeous). For color, we sell pink, blue, and gold-tone thermal rolls in the Creator Pack.
What if I don't love it?
Send it back within 30 days for a full refund. No restocking fees, no return shipping costs. We're confident it'll stay — but you're never locked in.
